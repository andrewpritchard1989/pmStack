Five modes

Pick the mode that fits your work

Each mode is a structured workflow. Skills chain together automatically.

New Feature

Build the right thing the right way

Start with real discovery. Map opportunities and hypotheses before touching a design. Gate on customer evidence before engineering starts.

Workflow

/pm-office-hours Discovery session + TE tree
/pm-problem-framing Deep problem decomposition
/pm-assumption-audit Risk & assumption mapping
/pm-cpo-review CPO challenge + web research
/pm-prototype Build + test with customers
Decision gate — proceed or pivot
/pm-spec-review PRD quality audit

When to use

✓
You have a vague idea or stakeholder request and need to shape it into a validated initiative
✓
You want to avoid building the wrong thing by jumping straight from goal to solution
✓
Engineering is asking for a spec and you haven't validated the core hypothesis yet
✓
You want a structured handoff doc with acceptance criteria, edge cases, and metrics baked in
Optimisation

Improve what you've already shipped

Start from data, not opinion. Identify the right lever to pull, design a rigorous experiment, and validate before scaling changes.

Workflow

/pm-office-hours Optimisation framing session
/pm-metrics-review Measurement plan audit
/pm-cpo-review Validate hypothesis & approach
/pm-trade-off-analysis Structured decision analysis
/pm-prototype Experiment design + test
/pm-post-launch-review After experiment: hypothesis validation

When to use

✓
A metric is underperforming and you need to diagnose the cause before designing a fix
✓
You want to run an A/B test and need a rigorous measurement plan with proxy metrics and counter-metrics
✓
You have multiple improvement options and need a structured framework to evaluate the trade-offs
✓
An experiment shipped and you need to capture learnings and decide the next step
Research

Understand before you commit

Structure a research sprint before committing to a direction. Explore the problem space, map the competitive landscape, and synthesise findings into clear next steps.

Workflow

/pm-office-hours Research framing session
/pm-cpo-review Challenge research framing
/pm-prototype Optional: concept test
/pm-competitive-intel Landscape analysis via browser
Synthesis — fork to Feature or Optimisation

When to use

✓
You're entering a new problem space and need to understand the landscape before proposing solutions
✓
Leadership has asked a strategic question and you need structured research to answer it credibly
✓
You want to run user interviews and need a structured guide based on your problem hypotheses
Strategy

Prioritise, plan, and communicate direction

Turn a pile of opportunities into a coherent roadmap. Score initiatives across multiple frameworks, stress-test the plan, and write the comms to get stakeholders aligned.

Workflow

/pm-office-hours Strategy framing session
/pm-cpo-review Challenge the direction
/pm-prioritisation ICE, RICE, opportunity scoring
/pm-roadmap-review Alignment, deps, capacity
/pm-comms-draft Launch announcements & updates
Break into initiatives → New Feature mode

When to use

✓
Planning season is approaching and you have more opportunities than capacity
✓
You need to present a roadmap to leadership and want it to be defensible against hard questions
✓
You need to communicate a direction change and want to tailor the message for different audiences
QBR

Walk into your QBR fully prepared

Build a narrative that connects your team's work to company goals. Simulate executive reactions, find the weak spots, and generate the final deliverable in your chosen format.

Full cycle workflow

/pm-qbr-context Audience & context brief
/pm-qbr-narrative Minto Pyramid narrative arc
/pm-metrics-review Validate metrics claims
/pm-qbr-stress-test Executive reaction simulation
/pm-qbr-red-team Adversarial review (6 lenses)
/pm-qbr-generate Output: deck, memo, or script

When to use

✓
Your quarterly business review is coming up and you need to build a narrative from raw data
✓
You have a draft deck and want to stress-test it before the exec sees it
✓
You need to connect team-level work to company OKRs in a way that actually lands with leadership
✓
You want the final output as a real .pptx file, a written memo, or a presenter script

Built on Thoughtful Execution

PMStack integrates Annina Koskinen's Thoughtful Execution framework (used at Spotify) into the New Feature mode. It enforces the discipline of working Goal → Data → Opportunities → Hypotheses → Solutions — preventing the most common PM failure mode: jumping straight from goal to solution.

Goal Measurable metric
Opportunity A Distinct problem space
Opportunity B Distinct problem space
Hypothesis A1 → A2 Multiple per opportunity
Solution designs Multiple per hypothesis

All skills

20 specialist commands

Each command is a structured role with a defined methodology and opinionated outputs.

/pm-office-hours Entry point
Discovery session
Start here. 4 modes: New Feature, Optimisation, Research, Strategy. Produces Product Brief and TE tree.
/pm-problem-framing New Feature
Problem decomposition
Deep problem analysis and customer segment definition. Maps the problem space before jumping to solutions.
/pm-assumption-audit New Feature
Risk & assumption map
Extracts every load-bearing assumption, rates them by risk, and designs tests for the riskiest ones.
/pm-cpo-review All modes
CPO challenge + research
Two-phase. Phase 1: opinionated challenge from a CPO lens. Phase 2: web research to back up or challenge the initiative.
/pm-prototype New Feature
Build & test
Generates Figma Make prompts or HTML prototypes, then auto-drafts a customer test plan from all prior artifacts.
/pm-spec-review New Feature
PRD quality audit
Systematic review against upstream user stories, acceptance criteria, and edge cases. Blocks low-quality specs before handoff.
/pm-plan-stakeholder-review New Feature
Stakeholder simulation
Simulates eng lead, design lead, and business lead perspectives to surface objections before the real meeting.
/pm-metrics-review Optimisation
Measurement plan audit
Reviews proxy metrics, baselines, counter-metrics, and success criteria. Catches measurement traps before experiments run.
/pm-trade-off-analysis Optimisation
Structured decision analysis
Captures the decision context, maps options and their trade-offs, and produces a decision record with clear rationale.
/pm-post-launch-review Optimisation
Post-launch analysis
Validates hypotheses against actual results, extracts learnings, and determines next steps after an experiment or launch.
/pm-competitive-intel Research
Competitive landscape
Uses the live browser to map competitors, positioning, and market dynamics. Not a static snapshot.
/pm-prioritisation Strategy
Multi-framework scoring
Scores initiatives across ICE, RICE, opportunity scoring, and cost of delay. Surfaces the order that actually makes sense.
/pm-roadmap-review Strategy
Roadmap integrity check
Reviews alignment to strategic priorities, dependency sequencing, and capacity realism. Flags the roadmap risks leadership will ask about.
/pm-comms-draft Strategy
Product communication
Drafts launch announcements, internal updates, changelogs, and stakeholder emails tailored to each audience.
/pm-qbr-context QBR
QBR context & audience
Start here for QBR prep. Gathers audience profile, executive incentives, and performance data into a context brief.
/pm-qbr-narrative QBR
Narrative arc builder
Connects team-level work to company goals using Minto Pyramid structure. Conclusion first, then the evidence.
/pm-qbr-stress-test QBR
Executive simulation
Adopts the exec's perspective from the Context Brief and challenges the narrative in real time.
/pm-qbr-red-team QBR
Adversarial review
Attacks the QBR narrative from six hostile lenses: data integrity, alternative interpretations, and more.
/pm-qbr-generate QBR
Final deliverable
Generates the output in your chosen format: PowerPoint deck (.pptx), written memo, or presenter script.
/browse Infrastructure
Live browser
Real Chromium browser for competitive research, authenticated testing, and live site verification.
